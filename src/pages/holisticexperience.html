Holistic Experience Artefact, Linked to Key Skills

During my WIL placement at AKA Studio, I sought out an industry mentor independently to gain deeper insights into the marketing and advertising sector. Connecting with a seasoned professional outside of the university framework provided a unique perspective on industry practices and career development.

My mentor, an experienced digital marketing strategist, guided me through various aspects of the industry, including client management, strategic planning, and campaign execution. Regular meetings with my mentor enhanced my understanding of real-world applications of theoretical concepts learned during my studies.

Through this mentorship, I developed critical skills such as effective communication, networking, and professional etiquette. My mentor emphasized the importance of building and maintaining professional relationships, which significantly improved my ability to interact confidently with clients and colleagues.

Additionally, I volunteered for a local non-profit organization during my placement period, where I applied my technical skills to enhance their digital marketing efforts. This experience required creativity, strategic planning, and analytical thinking as I managed their social media campaigns and created content to engage their audience effectively.

These holistic experiences during my placement not only broadened my professional network but also enhanced my transferable skills, such as leadership, teamwork, and problem-solving. These experiences have significantly contributed to my personal and professional development, preparing me for a successful career in the ICT industry.
